Favourite Cloud Service: Google Drive
About Cloud Storage
The beginning of cloud storage services, such as Google Drive and Dropbox, started a cloud computing revolution. In the years previous to this, we relied heavily on local data storage in the form of Hard Disk drives, and later Solid State drives. Since the introduction of these cloud storage services however, we have seen a shift in the way we store and transfer files and other data.
